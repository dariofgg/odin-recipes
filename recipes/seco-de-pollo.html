Seco De Pollo

Description

El Seco De Pollo is an easy and delicous ecuadorian dish. It's made with chicken and vegetables, so it's a healthy alternative to other chicken recipes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Green Pimiento
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. cut the tomato, the onion, and the green pimiento by half
  2. Trow the halfs in a blender. add a cup of water and blend it
  3. In a pot, put the chicken and pour the blender's content all together
  4. Cook the chicken at slow fire
  5. Serve the chicken with rice
  6. Enjoy
